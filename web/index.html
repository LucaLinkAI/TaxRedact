🛡️ Tax PDF Redactor

🔒 100% private — your file never leaves this device

Upload a tax-return PDF. Detected PII (SSN, EIN, names, address, phone, email, account numbers) is physically removed and a redacted copy downloads automatically. All processing happens right here in your browser — nothing is uploaded to any server.

⚠️ Redaction is heuristic — always review the output before sharing, especially for non-standard or scanned (image-based) PDFs, where text can't be searched.
